RE-IGNITE

VIRTUSA DISHA PROGRAMME

Return to the workforce the right way. We value your skills and knowledge – we’ll welcome you back and show you why VIRTUSA is a great place to re-start your tech career.

TEDxVirtusa Live on Sept 7, 2020

Working in VIRTUSA is always about impact, inclusion, and infinite experiences. There are even more great reasons to join the Re-Ignite program:

VIRTUSA's Tech Re-Entry Program is for experienced professionals who took a break from the workforce and are looking to restart their careers. This paid returnship offers a unique opportunity to re-build your skills through an array of well-curated learning programs, on-the-job projects, and access to the latest technologies and multi-disciplinary teams. While this program is for professionals who took a career break of at least two years, we have seen professionals who took as long as a 20-year break regain confidence through the program, excel in their new roles, and create real impact. Reigniting is a spark, a catalytic chain reaction that incites new ideas and curiosity and potential. Reigniting enlivens, invigorates, inspires. Reigniting connects people through ideas.

TEDxVirtusa 2020 will feature succes stories by people who have reignited their lives by following their dreams.
